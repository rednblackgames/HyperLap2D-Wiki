Share images with your friends! Send photos and pictures from your computer, phone or camera.

> Images are stored until you choose to remove them.

To share an image:

1. Open a message with someone
2. Select the **Send Media** button
3. Pick an image

Supported types:

* JPEG
* PNG
* BMP
* GIF

Unsupported types:

* SVG
* WEBP
